Questions, Answered

Who is this for?

High potential individuals, teams and organisations who want to eliminate growth constraints fast and create multiples of real value fast.

Is this therapy?

No. The programme uses evidence-based coaching methodologies — NLP, timeline therapy, IFS — but is not clinical treatment. If a clinical condition is identified at intake, appropriate referrals are made.

What do clients commit to?

Full attendance at all sessions, between-session assignments, and accountability protocols. The session room creates clarity. Life is where transformation happens.

How are sessions delivered?

In person wherever possible and during block weeks. Video call for remote sessions with no reduction in quality. Block weeks are always in person at agreed locations.
