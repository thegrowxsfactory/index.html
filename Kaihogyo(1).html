The Compounding Return

The Ancient Foundation

Why Kaihōgyō
Changes Everything

For over 1,000 years, the Marathon Monks of Mount Hiei have circled a sacred mountain — walking and running 24,000 miles over seven years in pursuit of enlightenment. They call it Kaihōgyō: the practice of circling the mountains.


The 10-X Career Transformation Programme is built on this exact philosophy. Your career is the mountain. Each module is a sacred station. Each week of practice, a circuit completed. You do not climb once and descend — you circle, deepen, and transform with every pass.


Unlike conventional programmes that deliver content and leave you to apply it, this journey demands your full presence. Like the gyoja (spiritual athlete), you will face discomfort, identity crisis, and breakthrough — all by design.

巤
Kaihō · Circling
Return to Deepen

You revisit core career challenges from new altitudes. Each pass reveals what was invisible before. There is no wasted ground — only deepening mastery.

行
Gyō · Daily Practice
Discipline Over Intensity

Transformation is built in daily micro-actions, not heroic one-off events. The monks walk before dawn. You build before the world wakes — consistently, quietly, powerfully.

千
Sennichi · 1,000 Days
The Long Game Wins

Compounding effort creates exponential results. The 10-X result is not achieved in a weekend — it emerges from sustained commitment that builds on itself, day after day.

不
Fudō · Immovability
Unshakeable Identity

The monks embody Fudō Myō-ō — the immovable deity. You will forge a professional identity so grounded that no external circumstance can destabilise your trajectory.

“
The mountain has always been waiting.
The question is whether you are ready to circle it.

The Supreme Trial

Dō-iri: The Professional Fast

At the heart of Kaihōgyō sits the Dō-iri — nine days without food, water, or sleep. The monk must transcend the body or perish. Most never face this trial. Those who do, become living saints. Only 46 monks have completed the full 1,000-day challenge since 1885.

Your 10-X programme contains its own Dō-iri moment: a deliberately constructed 90-day challenge that takes you beyond your current identity. It will feel like career death. It will be a rebirth. This is where 10-X lives.

The Gyoja Selection

This Path Is Not
For Everyone

The monks do not accept every applicant. Neither do we. The 10-X journey is for those at an inflection point — ready to commit fully, not sample curiously.

⧈
The Plateaued Professional

Competent, respected, and stuck. You've outgrown your current ceiling but lack the framework to break through to the next level of impact and compensation.

⚡
The Ambitious Pivotter

In transition — industry shift, role change, post-redundancy. You refuse to land at the same level you left. You want to use this disruption as a 10-X launch.

◌
The High-Performer Seeking Mastery

Already performing well but operating at 40% of your potential. You want the radical clarity and strategy that turns good into legendary.

⬡
The Return-to-Work Navigator

You've taken time away and are re-entering a changed landscape. You don't want your old career back — you want the 10-X version of what you're now capable of.

◆
The Leader in Waiting

You see the leadership role clearly but can't close the perception gap. This programme gives you the strategic visibility and brand to make the invisible undeniable.

★
The Burnout Rebuilder

You succeeded on others' terms and paid the price. You are rebuilding — this time on your own terms, toward a fundamentally more sustainable form of mastery.
